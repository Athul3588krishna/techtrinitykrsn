Instructions to Participate

  1. Registration:
    • Register online, Pay at the venue with a fee of ₹50.
  2. Competition Rules:
    • Participants will create a short instagram reel based on a provided theme.
    • The video should have a maximum duration of [specify time, e.g., 30-60 seconds].
    • You can use any video editing software of your choice (e.g., Adobe Premiere, Final Cut Pro, InShot, etc.).
    • You must submit the final video in [specify format, e.g., MP4, MOV].
  3. Event Format:
    • Participants will receive the theme at the start of the event.
    • Your video must be original (no pre-existing footage or templates).
    • The video must include the provided theme and be engaging for social media.
  4. Judging Criteria:
    • Creativity: How innovative and original the video is.
    • Editing Skills: Smoothness of transitions, effects, and overall production quality.
    • Relevance: How well the video fits the given theme.
    • Engagement: How engaging and appealing the video is to the audience (style, visuals, music).
  5. Winner Announcement:
    • The participant with the most impressive video reel will win ₹1000.
    • In case of a tie, the judges will consider additional factors such as creativity or audience feedback.
    • Judges’ decisions will be final and binding.
  6. Important Notes:
    • Participants must bring their own laptops and editing software (or use the provided systems, if available).
    • The final video must be submitted on time. Late submissions will not be accepted.
