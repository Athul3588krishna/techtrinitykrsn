CPU Assembling Competition

Event Details

  • Entry Fee: ₹100 per participant
  • Winner's Prize: ₹2000

Instructions to Participate:

  1. Registration:
    • Register online, pay at the venue with a fee of ₹100.
  2. Competition Rules:
    • Participants must assemble a fully functional CPU from the provided components.
    • All necessary parts (motherboard, processor, RAM, storage, PSU, etc.) will be provided on-site.
  3. Event Format:
    • The competition will be conducted in individual rounds.
    • Each participant will have a set time to assemble the CPU.
    • The competition will be judged based on speed and accuracy.
  4. Judging Criteria:
    • Time: How quickly the CPU is assembled and successfully booted.
    • Accuracy: Proper placement and connection of components.
    • Neatness: Cable management and overall assembly quality.
  5. Winner Announcement:
    • The participant who assembles and boots the CPU in the shortest time with accurate assembly will win ₹2000.
    • Judges’ decisions will be final and binding.
  6. Important Notes:
    • All components and tools (screwdrivers, thermal paste, etc.) will be provided.
    • Participants must handle components with care; any damage caused intentionally may lead to disqualification.
