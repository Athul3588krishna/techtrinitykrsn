eFootball (PES) Tournament Guidelines

Showcase your gaming skills, face off in exciting matches, and claim the championship prize!

Event Details

  • Entry Fee: ₹100 per participant
  • Winner's Prize: ₹2000

Instructions to Participate

  1. Registration:
    • Register online, Pay at the venue with a fee of ₹100.
  2. Competition Rules:
    • Matches will be conducted in a 1 vs 1 knockout format.
    • The loser of each match will be eliminated, and the winner advances to the next round.
    • The final winner will receive ₹2000.
  3. Match Format:
    • Game: eFootball (PES).
    • Platform: Android/iOS.
    • Match Duration: 5-10 minutes per match.
    • Teams: Players can select any club or national team from the available options.
    • Settings: Default game settings will be used unless stated otherwise by the organizers.
  4. Rules and Guidelines:
    • Participants must bring smartphones with the game installed and updated to the latest version.
    • Ensure your smartphone is sufficiently charged. Carry a power bank if needed.
    • Unsportsmanlike behavior, such as intentional disconnects or pausing, will result in disqualification.
    • No external mods, cheats, or scripts are allowed.
  5. Judging and Progression:
    • Matches will be monitored by event coordinators.
    • In case of a tie, the match will proceed to extra time and penalties if needed.
    • The winner of the final match will receive ₹2000.
  6. Important Notes:
    • Participants must follow the event coordinator's instructions during the tournament.
    • Players are responsible for ensuring their smartphones are compatible with the game.
