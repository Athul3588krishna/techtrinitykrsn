Typing Competition

Event Details

  • Entry Fee: ₹50 per participant
  • Winner's Prize: ₹1000

Instructions to Participate:

  1. Registration:
    • Register online, and pay the fee of ₹50 at the venue.
  2. Competition Rules:
    • The text to be typed will be provided at the start of the event.
    • Typists must type the exact text without errors. Each mistake will deduct points or time.
  3. Event Format:
    • Each participant will have a fixed time to type.
    • Scores will be based on speed, accuracy, and completion of the text.
  4. Equipment:
    • Computers and keyboards will be provided at the venue.
    • Judging Criteria:
      • Accuracy: Number of correctly typed characters.
      • Speed: Total time taken to complete the text.
      • Final Score: Calculated based on the above two factors.
    • Winner Announcement:
      • The participant with the highest score will win ₹1000.
      • In case of a tie, a tie-breaker round will decide the winner.
    • Important Notes:
      • Arrive at least 10 minutes before the competition begins.
      • Maintain decorum and follow the event coordinator's instructions.
